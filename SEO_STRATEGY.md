# JPD Complete Electrical - SEO Strategy & Keywords

This document outlines the Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategy for JPD Complete Electrical, based on the current website architecture, content, and brand guidelines.

## 1. Core Objectives
*   **Establish Domain Authority**: Position JPD Complete Electrical as the "Reliable, Honest, Professional" choice in Adelaide.
*   **Local Dominance**: Target "Adelaide" specific search terms to capture high-intent local traffic.
*   **Audience Targeting**: Focus on three key segments:
    1.  **Homeowners** (Renovations, Repairs, Lighting)
    2.  **Builders** (New builds, Reliable subcontracting)
    3.  **Property Managers** (Maintenance, Compliance, Fast turnaround)

## 3. On-Page SEO Strategy

### Metadata Configuration
*   **Title Tag Format**: `JPD Complete Electrical | Professional Electrician Adelaide`
    *   *Strategy*: Brand Name + Primary Keyword + Location.
*   **Meta Description**: "Reliable. Honest. Professional. Electrical Work You Can Trust. Adelaide's trusted electrician for builders, property managers, and homeowners. We show up, clean up, and keep our promises."
    *   *Strategy*: Includes core pillars, target audience, and unique selling proposition (USP).

### Content Hierarchy
*   **H1 Tags**: Used strictly for the main page title (e.g., "Reliable. Honest. Professional. Electrical Work You Can Trust.").
*   **H2/H3 Tags**: Structure content by service offerings (e.g., "Our Services", "Why Choose JPD") to help crawlers understand content relevance.
*   **Semantic HTML**: Usage of `<section>`, `<article>`, and proper `alt` tags for images (e.g., "Justin - JPD Complete Electrical Owner") to improve accessibility and image search ranking.

## 4. Technical SEO
*   **Structured Data (JSON-LD)**: Implemented to help search engines understand the business type, location, and services.
*   **Performance**:
    *   Next.js App Router for optimized server-side rendering (SSR).
    *   `next/image` for automatic image optimization and lazy loading.
    *   Fast page load times (Core Web Vitals focus).
*   **Mobile Optimization**: Fully responsive design with touch-friendly navigation ("Sticky Mobile CTA") to favor mobile-first indexing.

## 5. Local SEO & Trust Signals
*   **Google Business Profile**: Optimization of the Google Review link and display of "5-Star Google Rated" badge on the homepage.
*   **Social Proof**: Integration of client reviews and "Featured Projects" (e.g., specific jobs like "Medical Clinic Upgrade") to demonstrate local expertise and activity.
*   **Contact Accessibility**: Clear, clickable phone numbers (`tel:`) and "Get a Quote" CTAs sticky on mobile.

## 6. Future Content Opportunities
To further improve rankings, the following content expansions are recommended:
*   **Location Pages**: Create specific pages for suburbs (e.g., "Electrician Glenelg", "Electrician Norwood").
*   **Blog/Insights**: Articles on "Electrical Safety Tips", "Energy Saving with LEDs", or "Understanding Switchboard Upgrades" to capture informational queries.
*   **Case Studies**: Detailed pages for the "Featured Projects" with more text content describing the problem and solution.
